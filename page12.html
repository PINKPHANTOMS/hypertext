Laissez-Faire Affaire

A city, within a city
Under a state, run by authority
Driven by money, talking money
Donations from, Corporations Inc.

And high-class handicap
That lack the sympathy
To see the suffering
Brought on by a corrupt dream

Starting so innocent, turned to magnificent
As they would grow they would come to know
Our laissez-faire
Our laissez-faire affaire

As if we’ve, retrogressed
Back to antiquity
As if we’re ruling by fear
And anxiety

Yesterday, today,
We’ve always been the same..
